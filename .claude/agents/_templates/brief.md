- **Date:** [YYYY-MM-DD]
- **Objective:** [the business goal — not the task. Why does this matter to the client/business?]
- **Inputs:** [links/paths to upstream artifacts this agent needs]
- **Constraints:** [stack, brand tokens, budget, deadline, platform, compliance]
- **Acceptance criteria:** [how the receiving agent knows it is done — testable]
